My Story

I'm a student in my last Semester of my Bachelor in Mediadesign. Often on my journey I needed Icons for projects - not only for uni projects but also privately. This made me realize there are sources with free icons (even commercially!) but there are often strings or fine print, which holds you back. That's why I created Minimaly. For Students, Startups, Freelancers & everything in between. To have a reliable Source of 100% free icons.
